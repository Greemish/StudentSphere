Think of a struggling student , a student in need of academic assistance , a student that doesn’t 
know where to consult. That is where we as the Student Sphere (AI-Powered Academic Ecosystem) 
developers step in. Our mission is to design a comprehensive platform that connects students, 
mentors, and lecturers in a unified digital environment , aimed at increasing academic 
performance by providing personalized learning experiences through artificial intelligence. It 
monitors and tracks student progress and understanding to ensure continuous academic 
improvement. Additionally, the platform promotes collaboration and peer mentorship, creating an 
interactive and supportive educational community. 
It will be developed using JAVA, JavaScript, HTML , CSS and MySql database.
